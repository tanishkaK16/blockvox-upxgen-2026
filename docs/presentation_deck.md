## Slide 3: The Tech Stack (Under the Hood)
**How it works**:
- **Merkle Tree Whitelisting**: Admin submits a root, voters submit a proof. 0% database exposure.
- **Commit-Reveal Flow**: Ballots are encrypted on-chain until the reveal phase. No front-running.
- **Avalanche C-Chain**: Low-gas, high-speed execution. Optimized for mass adoption.
